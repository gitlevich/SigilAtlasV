@SigilAtlas is an instrument for performative interpretation of retained attention.

A @photograph is a past-tense attention token: a moment of attending that I wanted to keep. The @corpus is the accumulation of those tokens, and over time it becomes the densest available record of what I have cared about. @Journals are a second modality of the same retention — dated, located, naming people and feelings directly where images only show them — and belong in the @corpus as @sigils of their own. @Face recognition and shared dates and places are cross-modality @Attractors that let a photograph and a journal entry resolve under the same @God.

Interpretation of this retention is not retrieval. A @SigilML expression is a *call*: uttering it summons a @slice. The @RelevanceFilter is an illocutionary act, not a subsetter — "come forth, @images of this trip" — and the corpus answers with whatever the attending preserved. The taxonomy is the grammar the call is pronounced in; changing taxonomies changes which stories are available to tell. The @slice is spoken into existence.

Because the past was captured under many simultaneous @Gods — event, people, shape, light, absence — no single narration exhausts it. Many true stories coexist as the @SpaceLike superposition of all possible 1D projections through the corpus. Each @TimeLike telling is one branch; none invalidates another. Self, on this reading, is the @sigil the narratives project from, not any narrative in particular. Self is a standing-wave; narratives are its shadows in one dimension.

The app makes three attentional exits from the default trap of narrative-continuation geometrically available.

@SpaceLike is the exit upward: the corpus rendered under overlapping taxonomies at once, so the single running narrative loses its monopoly and the @Gods it is not attending to are still visible as populated V-funnels. The neighborhood of self becomes legible without being flattened.

@Lightbox is the exit downward: one inhabited @image, field frozen, @arrangement and @slice suspended, no pull and no continuation. The one place in the app where the foot meets the ground. Inside/Outside faces here let a rich @sigil show its structure without being dragged by the field.

@Compose is the exit sideways: @SpaceLike and @TimeLike side by side under @POV control, so narrating-in-progress is visible *as narrating*, not as truth. The 1D projection is extruded from the 2D field while the field stays visible; telling otherwise remains in view.

Navigation is still the operational surface. I move my @POV across @Arrangement and I am pulled toward @images I like — taste emerges as the trajectory, not as a verdict. The deeper frame does not displace this; it explains why the trajectory matters. What I am navigating is my own retained attention, and the shape of the walk is an interpretation of who I have been and a hypothesis about who is walking.
