# Sigil

is a recursive structure with these properties:

- its boundaries are defined by its @invariant: a predicate constraining the inside of the @sigil
- it each of its @affordances is a #functor over the domain covered by the predicate
- it is #named and recognizable by @attention
- it is !recognizable by @name
- it is held by other @sigils under @Roles — local position-terms drawn from role-taxonomies, distinct from its globally-unique @Name

See @AttentionLanguage@Sigil for more.

It is the central concept of this application as both this specification and the model are based on it.
