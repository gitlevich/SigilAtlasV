## Partner

I’d place Torus directly under Explore as one of its primary children, not buried under UI and not off in a general ontology area. In other words, Explore owns the product, and Torus owns the spatial-temporal geometry that Explore depends on.

Then the other core sigils under Explore would hang off that division of responsibility. Torus defines coordinates, wrapping, neighborhoods, strips, slices, and timelike versus spacelike relations, while sibling sigils under Explore define things like preference learning, filter state, and visual projection that consume Torus rather than redefining it.
