@Torus is already correctly positioned directly under @Explore as a first-class primary child: not buried under UI and not off in a general ontology area. If @Explore is the product root, then @Torus is its owned spatial-temporal geometry and the source of geometric truth that @Explore and other sigils should defer to, defining the core contracts they depend on: coordinates, wrapping, neighborhoods, strips, slices, zoom, position, and traversal. Many hard contracts should therefore live in @Torus or explicitly defer to it from within @Explore, even if the spec is not yet fully consistent about that. @Torus also grounds timelike versus spacelike relations through @Timelike and @Spacelike.
