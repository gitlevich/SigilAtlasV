# Sigil Proximity

Distance from the @sigil I am wearing to another. !only-defined-for-neighbor-sigils.

Either enumerated or range.

Enumerated: a finite set of discrete states. Absent/present is an enumeration with two values. Absent/single/flock is an enumeration with three. If a @sigil is present, its children may be enumerations within it: a person's age is an enumeration (infant, child, teenager, adult, elderly), so is clothing (formal, casual, uniform).

Range: a continuous value from 0 to 1. How much of a quality is present. How red, how bright, how serene.

The type follows from the nature of the thing. Subjects are enumerated. Qualities are range.

I can define Manhattan @sigil by constraining the geo boundaries of an area in New York, spring @sigil as the range of dates in March, April and May, papa and mama @sigils as the people who I recognize as my parents, and rabbit @sigil I recognize as a rabbit.

Proximity then means: for Manhattan, how far I am geographically. For Spring, the distance is temporal. For my parents, the distance is physical, and so is for rabbits.

Then I can define a @corpus @slice as a @sigil containing only @images with my papa and mama, no rabbits, taken in spring in Manhattan.
