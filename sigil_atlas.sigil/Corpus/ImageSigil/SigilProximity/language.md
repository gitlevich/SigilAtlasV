# Other Sigil Proximity

Distance from the @sigil I am wearing to another. 

I can define Manhattan @sigil by constraining the geo boundaries of an area in New York, spring @sigil as the range of dates in March, April and May, papa and mama @sigils as the people who I recognize as my parents, and rabbit @sigil I recognize as a rabbit. 

Proximity then means: for Manhattan, how far I am geographically. For Spring, the distance is temporal. For my parents, the distance is physical, and so is for rabbits.

Then I can define a @corpus @slice as a @sigil containing only @images with my papa and mama, no rabbits, taken in spring in Manhattan.
