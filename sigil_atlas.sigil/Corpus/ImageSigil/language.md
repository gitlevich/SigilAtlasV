# Image Sigil

A @sigil with the boundary defined by the @image's @MajorContrasts and @SigilProximitys. 

Its @invariants are to !include-major-image-contrasts and !constrain-relevant-sigil-proximity. 

Its @affordances are to expose @image@identity, #image, #image-metadata and #embeddings.

To #wrap-an-image, we use @ImageWrappingAlgorithm.
