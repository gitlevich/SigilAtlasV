# Synthetic Image

An image that shows why the members of this @ImageNeighborhoodSigil belong together. Synthesized from the member @images and their #embeddings. 

Has a @shape, @texture, @color. Visually representative of an image neighborhood.

It is !convergent — as the neighborhood narrows to a single @ImageSigil, the synthetic image becomes that @image.
