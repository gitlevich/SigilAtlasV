# Image Sigil Neighborhood

A @sigil with the boundary containing either other @ImageNeighborhoodSigils or @ImageSigils. Unless it answers the question #is-root yes, it is contained within another @ImageNeighborhoodSigil and is surrounded by other similar neighborhoods in their @ImageNeighborhoodSigils.

It must be !recognizable by its "overhead" #synthetic-image synthesized in some repeatable way.
