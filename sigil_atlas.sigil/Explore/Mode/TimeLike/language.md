# Time-like

The @torus arranged as a continuous timeline. @Time flows left-to-right within each @strip and top-to-bottom across strips — like reading a book. The strip wraps: the end meets the beginning. I can spin it — #playback moves through the @corpus.

Within a strip, @images are continuous in @time. Adjacent strips are close in time — the strip above is nearer than two strips above. The @torus flattens to a square so horizontal and vertical distance feel proportional.

@Stories are detected at the current @timescale. Zooming the timescale regroups images into coarser or finer temporal clusters.
