# Time-like

The @torus arranged as film strips ordered by @time. I can spin it — #playback moves through the @corpus as a timeline.

Each @strip is a @story — a burst of @images close in @time. @Time flows left-to-right within every strip. @Stories stack vertically — older above, newer below. All strips move in sync during #playback.

The visual rhythm of the @strips reflects the temporal rhythm of the @corpus. Dense shooting periods produce long strips. Quiet periods produce short strips.

@Stories are detected at the current @timescale. Zooming the timescale collapses fine stories into coarser ones or splits coarse stories into finer ones.
