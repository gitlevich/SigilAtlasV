# Time-like

The @torus ordered by @time.

Each @strip is a @story — a burst of @images close in @time. @Time flows left-to-right within every strip. @Stories stack vertically — older above, newer below. All strips move in sync when scrolling horizontally.

The visual rhythm of the @strips reflects the temporal rhythm of the @corpus. Dense shooting periods produce long strips (many images). Quiet periods produce short strips (few images).

@Stories are detected at the current @timescale. Zooming the timescale collapses fine stories into coarser ones or splits coarse stories into finer ones.
