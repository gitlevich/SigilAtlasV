# Space-like

In this mode I make sense of the @images spatially. I would like to see similar images near each other, arranged in local @neighborhoods clustering together. Similarity is initially what the model believes is similar. 

In @OrderedMode, I impose an order on the images in the @slice so they appear in that order, i.e. by time. Or orange to blue.

In @NeighborhoodMode, the images huddle together in !local-neighborhoods



- name @attractors I want to include in the @slice
- constrain the @slice by defining @contrasts I care about and choosing ranges I prefer. 

The result is the @slice.

With no contrasts configured, the @slice is the entire @corpus and the layout uses raw @embedding distance from the active @visionModel.

!local-neighborhoods.
