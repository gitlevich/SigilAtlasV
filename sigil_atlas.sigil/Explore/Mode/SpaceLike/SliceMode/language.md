# Slice Mode

Shape the @slice from the @corpus using @contrast controls. Two tools:

#filter-by-range: set a range on a @contrast. Images outside the range are excluded.
#filter-by-proximity: name things I want to be closer to or farther from. Images are scored by distance to those named things — closer to what I want more of, farther from what I want less of.

Both compose. Multiple range filters AND together. Multiple proximity filters combine into a composite score. Together they narrow the @corpus into a @slice.
