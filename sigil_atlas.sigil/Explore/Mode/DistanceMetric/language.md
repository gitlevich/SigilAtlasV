# Distance Metric

What "close" and "far" mean on the @torus surface. @SpaceLike uses it to define neighborhoods. @TasteLike uses it to arrange around an @attractor.

Four metrics:

- **Embedding**: distance in the @contrastSpace defined by active @contrasts.
- **Temporal**: distance between capture dates.
- **Physical**: distance between GPS coordinates of capture locations.
- **Composite**: a combination. Weights to be determined by interaction.

@Similarity is the inverse of distance — what it means for two @images to be near each other.
