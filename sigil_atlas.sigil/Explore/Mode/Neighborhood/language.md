# Neighborhood

Regions of the @torus where images are similar by the active @distanceMetric. Not an explicit data structure — they emerge from the @similarity ordering.

A neighborhood has no hard boundary. It is a density: a region where nearby images share properties. At its center lives an @attractor — the point from which similarity radiates. Moving across the @torus is moving between neighborhoods.

The @pointOfView determines which neighborhood I am in. Zooming out reveals the neighborhood structure. Zooming in enters one neighborhood and shows individual images.
