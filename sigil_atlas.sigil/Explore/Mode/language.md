# Mode

A mode determines how @images from a @slice are ordered on the @torus. Three modes:

- @timelike: ordered by @time. Navigate through the temporal history of the @corpus.
- @spacelike: ordered by @similarity. Navigate through @Neighborhood.
- @tastelike: ordered by relevance to a @RelevanceFilter. Navigate toward what you find beautiful.

Switching modes recomputes the @strip layout. The @images in the current @slice stay the same — only their positions change. The transition should be smooth: images animate from their old positions to their new ones.
