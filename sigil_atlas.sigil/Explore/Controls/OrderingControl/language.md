# Ordering Control

Lets me define an @ordering by picking one or more @contrasts and their priority. The first @contrast is the primary sort axis; subsequent @contrasts break ties in sequence. Each @contrast in the @ordering has a direction — ascending or descending along the axis.

Visually, a vertical list of @contrasts with priority implied by order. #add-contrast to include one from the @ContrastControl library scoped to the current @sigil. #rearrange by dragging to change priority. #flip-direction to invert an axis. X icon to #delete the whole @OrderingControl (with confirmation).
