# Strip Assignment

How @images from a @slice get placed into @strips on the @torus. Depends on the active @mode.

In @timelike mode: each @story becomes a strip. @Time flows left-to-right. Stories stack vertically — older above, newer below. All strips scrub in sync.

In @spacelike mode: images sorted by visual similarity fill strips left-to-right. @Neighborhood emerge from the ordering.

In @tastelike mode: images sorted by relevance to the @relevanceFilter fill strips left-to-right. Most relevant images appear first.

!deterministic.
