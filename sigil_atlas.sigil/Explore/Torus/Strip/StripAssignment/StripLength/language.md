# Strip Length

The horizontal extent of a @strip on the @torus.

In @timelike mode, strip length is determined by the @story — it holds as many images as the story contains. Different strips have different lengths.

In @spacelike and @tastelike modes, strip length is a parameter that controls the torus aspect ratio. Can be derived from @frame width or set explicitly. The overall @torus should stay roughly square for comfortable navigation.
