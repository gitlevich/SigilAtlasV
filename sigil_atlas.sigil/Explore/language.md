# Explore

Let me explore the images in a number of different ways:

- @time-like
- @space-like
- @taste-like



When I give the @corpus a @RelevanceFilter, it responds with a @slice containing relevant images it had. 

I can traverse a @slice by specifying @slice#time-direction and expect to see a @slice!ordered sequence of @ImageSigils sorted in that direction to appear in my @frame.
