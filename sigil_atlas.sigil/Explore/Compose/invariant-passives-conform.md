The non-driving panes recompute from the driver's state. When @Source drives, @Program and @Timeline follow the @SigilML expression being authored. When @Program drives, @Source stays put (it represents the @corpus, which does not change) and @Timeline re-orders to the new arrangement. When @Timeline drives, @Source stays put and @Program's ambient tracks the playhead. Passive panes never animate independently of the driver; a pane that is not driving is a projection, not an authorer.
