# Atlas

The @Explore mode in which one @sigil's *structure* is inhabited — children, neighbors, gods, affordances, invariants, boundary, scope — as distinct from its pixels. Home of the @DesktopMetaphor's two faces: Inside (flat canvas) and Outside (3D landscape of funnels). A view-from-my-@POV throughout.

Sibling to @Lightbox. @Lightbox inhabits an @image as image — pixels at 100%, no structure. @Atlas inhabits any @sigil as sigil. An @image has both doors.

Default inhabited @sigil on entry is @self. Entering @Atlas on another @sigil is done by walking through a @Neighbor, descending into a @God, or selecting a @sigil from the current @Frame.

Low-resolution for now. Full articulation to land when the mode is built.
