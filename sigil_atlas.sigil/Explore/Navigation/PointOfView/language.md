# Point Of View

The camera. Three values: x, y (position on @torus surface, wraps) and z (distance from surface).

At z=min: the @frame shows three rows of @images. Below that threshold, further zoom leaves the field and enters the @Lightbox on the @image under the cursor. At z=max: entire @torus visible at micro-thumbnail resolution. Pan changes x, y. Zoom changes z. All continuous within the field.

!fixed-fov.
