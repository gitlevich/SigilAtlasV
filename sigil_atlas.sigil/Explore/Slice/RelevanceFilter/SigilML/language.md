# SigilML

Sigil Manipulation Language. A small composable language of predicates and ranges over @things and @contrasts.

Predicates: AND, OR, NOT. Operands: a @thing (membership), a @contrast range (a range between two polar @things). A @contrast itself is a range between @things.

A @RelevanceFilter is a SigilML expression. So is any compound @thing or compound @contrast — same language describes all composition in the app.
