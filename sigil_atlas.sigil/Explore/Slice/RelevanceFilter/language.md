# Relevance Filter

Defines the @invariants of the @slice — the predicate every @image in the @slice must satisfy. Expressed in @SigilML: a composable language of boolean predicates (AND, OR, NOT) over @things and @contrast ranges (a range between @things).

Two faces of one sigil. Used as a #query affordance, I actively ask for @images matching it. Used as an invariant, it declares what must hold inside the @slice.
