# Relevance Filter

Defines the @invariants of the @slice in terms of admitted ranges along the relevant @contrasts and @attractors I want to see. @Attractors define the what of the @slice; @contrasts constrain it further.

Two faces of one sigil. Used as an #query affordance, I actively ask for @images matching it. Used as an invariant, it declares what must hold inside the @slice.
