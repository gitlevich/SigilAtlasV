Pan (drag / arrow keys): move @PointOfView x,y along the @torus surface. Wraps at edges.
Zoom (scroll / pinch): move @PointOfView z closer to or further from the surface.
Select (click): choose an @image, show details in @leftPanel.
Focus (double-click): center @PointOfView on an @image. In @tastelike mode, also sets "more like this".
