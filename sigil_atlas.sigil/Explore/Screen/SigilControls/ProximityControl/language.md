# Proximity Control

A shorthand for a @contrastControl with role=attract and one defined pole.

The opposite pole is implicit — "not this." The concept group I type becomes pole A. Pole B is the anti-direction. The slider controls how much pull toward A (positive) or away from A (negative).

Equivalent to a @contrastControl where:
- Pole A = embed(concept group)
- Pole B = -embed(concept group)
- Role = attract
- Band = full width (no filtering, only scoring)

Exists as a convenience widget — quicker to set up than a full contrast control when I just want "more of this" or "less of that."
