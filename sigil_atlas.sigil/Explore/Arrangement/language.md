# Arrangement

How @images are laid out on the @surface given the current @slice. Two kinds, distinguished by the shape of attention they support: @SpaceLike arranges by @similarity as an ambient field (all-at-once apprehension); @TimeLike arranges by @sequencing along @time as a narrative (frame-by-frame reading). Each mode is a @SigilML expression that operates on the @slice — SpaceLike admits `Attract` operators over atoms; TimeLike admits `Bandpass` and `OrderBy` operators. Changing the arrangement rewrites the expression; evaluating it produces the layout.
