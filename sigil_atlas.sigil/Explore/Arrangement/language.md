# Arrangement

How @images are laid out on the @surface given the @slice. 

Two orthogonal axes:

*Attention-shape*: @SpaceLike or @TimeLike.
*@Perspective*: @perspectival and @aperspectival.

A @TimeLike rendering is a 1D projection of the taxonomies; @SpaceLike is the superposition of all such projections. @Compose makes both visible at once under @POV control.
