the 2D layout on the @torus must preserve @proximity from @contrastspace. @images close in @contrastspace must be close on the @torus; strips that span the full width smear proximity horizontally and violate this — an @image close to another in @contrastspace should not land far from it on the surface. This is the hardest invariant of @SpaceLike, and what breaks most naive strip-packed implementations.
