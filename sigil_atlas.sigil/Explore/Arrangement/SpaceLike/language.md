# Space-like

In this mode I make sense of the @images spatially. I can look at the entire @corpus or define its @slice in terms of @attractors and @contrasts.

I can enjoy my @slice either ordered using an @ordering or clustered into @neighborhoods by @similarity. 

## Building blocks

To shape a @slice, I need to #define-controls to manipulate @attractors, @contrasts and @ordering. 

I can use the @controls I defined to either arrange the @slice into @neighborhoods by @similarity, or by @ordering.
