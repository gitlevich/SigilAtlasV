# Similarity

How close two @images are in @contrastspace. Measured by @proximity — along the @contrasts I have named relevant, and as distance to any @attractor I have designated. Unnamed @contrasts are in superposition and do not contribute.

I define similarity by naming @contrasts — by color, by brightness, by mood — or by designating @attractors, whether @things or a concrete @TargetImage I pointed at.

In each @neighborhood, @images arrange by @proximity: closer is more similar. When multiple @attractors are active, neighborhoods overlap smoothly.

If I name nothing, similarity defaults to the active @visionModel's native @embedding distance.
