# Attractor

A point on the @torus around which @images arrange. Anchored in @contrastspace by either a @thing or a @TargetImage. Acts as a gravitational mass: @images feel its pull based on their @proximity — closer is stronger.

When multiple @attractors are active, each @image feels the sum of all pulls and settles at the equilibrium. A @neighborhood forms around each @attractor, and neighborhoods overlap smoothly where pulls are comparable.
