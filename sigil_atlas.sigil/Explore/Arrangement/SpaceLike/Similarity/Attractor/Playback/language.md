# Playback

Transport controls for moving through @time on the @torus. Modeled on JKL editing controls.

L: #play-forward. Each press doubles speed (1x, 2x, 4x, 8x, 16x).
J: #play-reverse. Same acceleration in reverse.
K: #stop.
K+L held together: #step-forward. One small step along @time.
K+J held together: #step-backward. One small step back.
Space: toggle between #play-forward at last speed and #stop.

During playback, the @torus scrolls horizontally — @time advances across all @strips in sync. The @POV stays fixed; the surface moves under it.
