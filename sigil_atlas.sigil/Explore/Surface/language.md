# Surface

The continuous space on which an @arrangement of @images lives. Always a @Torus — flat rectangle with wrapping on both axes. The @surface may optionally carry a @Relief (an elevation function derived from the arrangement's structure) and a @Lighting (the shape of attention illuminating it). Without @Relief, the surface is perfectly flat; without @Lighting, it is ambiently lit by default.
