# Stories

A story is a sequence of images taken close in time. Stories are recursive: I can take several images during a walk and have a few walks during the day. In images during one walk, just based on the timestamps, is one story, but the entire day is another. Or maybe I didn't take pictures for a few days and went and did that every day for three days. So that's another story.

And then it aggregates in a story which says, hey, over some longer period of time, I had this several days bursts followed by several days lulls and so forth. In other words, there are also sigils only grouped into neighborhoods along the time axis.
