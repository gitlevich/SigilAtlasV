# Lightbox

Where I inhabit one @image without the rest of the @slice pulling at me. The @arrangement and @navigation are suspended; there is no field, only this. The same @image exists in @SpaceLike and in @TimeLike, and the @Lightbox is the point at which the two modes coincide — inhabit it from either, and the other is what I return to.

I #enter the @Lightbox by zooming past the last step in the field. I #walk by lattice adjacency on the suspended @arrangement. I #toggle-metadata to see what the @image knows about itself. I #exit back to the @Frame I came from, untouched.

!field-frozen. !single-connection-point.
