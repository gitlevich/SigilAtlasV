While the @Lightbox is open, the @slice, the @arrangement, and the @control do not recompute. The field is in superposition — nothing is attending to it, so nothing is working it. When I #exit, the @Frame is bit-for-bit what it was at the moment I #entered.

This is not an implementation shortcut. It is the shape of attention in this app: there is only one attention, mine, and when it is on the @Lightbox it is not on the field.
