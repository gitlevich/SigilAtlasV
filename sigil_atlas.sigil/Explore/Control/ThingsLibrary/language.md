# Things Library

My personal vocabulary of @things, accumulated as I name concepts during exploration. Every @thing I have typed — into @AttractorControl, as a @contrast pole, as an @ordering axis — lives here until I explicitly remove it.

The library sits above @AttractorControl in the controls panel. Two faces of one relationship: @AttractorControl shows @things I have activated right now; @ThingsLibrary shows every @thing I have ever named. Pressing × on a pill in @AttractorControl #deactivates it — it moves here, not to the void. Pressing × here #deletes it for real, guarded by a confirmation.

I #activate a @thing by clicking it — the pill moves back to @AttractorControl and the field re-arranges. I #search within the library to find a @thing by name when I have accumulated many. When I want screen space, I #fold the library to a thin horizontal bar.

The library is !persistent across sessions — it belongs to the @Workspace. The sigils I name are mine.
