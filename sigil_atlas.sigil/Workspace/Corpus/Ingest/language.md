# Ingest

The process of bringing @images from an external location into the @corpus.

To ingest @images from a @Source, I specify one and #run-pipeline-from-source. The @pipeline runs against it, and the @corpus gets populated as a side effect. While it runs, the @pipeline #extracts-metadata, #builds-previews-and-thumbnails, and #embeds.
