# Journal

A second modality of retained attention, parallel to @image. A @journal entry is a dated, located, name-bearing @sigil: its @Boundary is the entry's start and end; its @Children are the @sigils it names by @Role; its @Gods are the taxonomies the entry attends (dates, places, people); its prose is itself. Inhabiting a @journal entry in @Lightbox uses the same Inside/Outside grammar as any other @sigil.

Cross-modality @Attractors link journals to images: a person-@sigil pulls both the @images where their face is recognized and the @journal entries where their name is written. A @slice drawn under such an attractor admits both kinds uniformly.

Low-resolution for now. @Ingest, storage shape, cross-linking affordances, and authoring surface are placeholders to be articulated when @journal becomes a real feature.
