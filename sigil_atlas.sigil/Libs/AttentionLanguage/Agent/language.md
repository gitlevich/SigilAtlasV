# Agent

Me in @TimeLike mode — @POV wearing a @Self and projecting a @narrative to keep @Self coherent across frames. Analogous to @EcologicalPsychology@Organism.

While attending @TimeLike, my @attention is locked on @Self's @invariants and traces patterns across @frames. I can attend to the entire frame at low @resolution or zoom into a section at high resolution — but not both. The @invariants induce a @goal: choose next frames to keep @observations within the @invariants. I remember the sequence of frames as a @narrative and interpret it as time. I project the shape of @Self forward to keep the narrative coherent — most of what that projection illuminates is imaginary, generated futures that will never become pattern.

Narrative capture: all attention locked on the narrative, unable to attend to the rest of the frame. This is the extreme of @TimeLike allocation — the ambient goes dark so the beam can stay bright.

When I allocate @attention to @SpaceLike instead, I am not an Agent — I am @POV at rest, apprehending the @frame whole, immobile in time. Agent and POV-at-rest are two facets of the same I, distinguished only by which attention mode is live. As an agent, I am. All else follows.
