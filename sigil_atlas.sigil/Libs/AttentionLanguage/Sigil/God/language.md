# gods (small g: the laws of men)

When @neighbors agree, to have a coherent social structure, on a set of rules they abide by and mutually enforce, they name a @sigil into existence and worship it with their @attention. That's the "god", or the law of men. 

Examples: The Law. Apple, Inc. The United States of America. The Catholic Church. Software Engineering. The English Language. The Future. Self.
