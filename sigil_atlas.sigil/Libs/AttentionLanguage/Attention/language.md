# Attention

The given. Precondition for first-person point of view. Finite — bounded capacity for @observation at any @resolution.

Attention is light. Its budget is fixed; every unit aimed in one direction is absent from the other. The @POV allocates between ambient (illuminating what is present on the @surface as a whole) and directional (illuminating generated futures projected forward by the @agent as @narrative). Most of what directional light illuminates never manifests — projection is largely hallucinatory in the neutral sense, and the @narrative is the thin line of futures that did.
