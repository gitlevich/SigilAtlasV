# Sigil Engineering

The practice of transforming a @narrative hierarchy into a @sigil.

Each @narrative enumerating a series of decisions to solve a problem in a given domain traces a trajectory over the surface of the domain's structure in @ContrastSpace. 

The more @narratives, the closer I can approximate this structure where it matters for my class of problems. 

Eventually, I see the shape of the domain: a surface on which all my starting points and solution live. The shape is a map: I can choose a trajectory from origin to destination as I please instead of having to navigating step by step.

A person who sees the shape of the domain is domain expert. 

A person merely following procedures is proficient but vulnerable to local changes of terrain: too easy to get lost.
