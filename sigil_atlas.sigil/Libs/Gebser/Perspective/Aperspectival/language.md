# Aperspectival

The fifth structure of consciousness in @Gebser: integral, post-@Perspective, trans-mental. Many @POV co-present, none privileged; @Superposition of @contrasts in polar opposition. @Perspective integrated, not erased.

Distinct from @Archaic, @Magical, @Mythical — those are pre-@Perspective; @Aperspectival is post-@Perspective.

Computational analog: trained-from-everywhere readings aggregating across @POV, privileging none. @attention is finite: @Aperspectival reaches only overview @Resolution; meaning past the overview requires @Perspective again.

A discipline, not a default.
