# Desktop Metaphor

Two interface-faces for inhabiting one @sigil. Home is @Atlas. Icons fitted for fitness, not for truth. View-from-my-@POV. Counterpart is @Aperspectival.

**Inside.** Flat canvas. @God is a downward V — a funnel seen edge-on. @Neighbor is a door-glyph. @Children are round, each a deterministic hue from @Name; the hue propagates into prose. @Boundary and @scope appear alongside. @affordances along the top; @invariants along the bottom. Up-arrow at top-left ascends. Two @Children named in one sentence are joined by a labeled arc.

**Outside.** 3D, porous. Each @sigil a sphere with holes; @Children smaller spheres inside. @Entanglement is proximity. A @God is a funnel, depth its pull. The Inside's V is this funnel edge-on.

Faces toggle per @sigil. Default inhabited @sigil is @self.
